About Us

Curating the best halal eateries in the Maryland, D.C, and NoVa area.

We started this concept for fun and feel blessed to have grown a following of inclusive and supportive Halal foodie enthusiasts. Through this journey, we’ve met the most amazing local business owners and look forward to continue reviewing restaurants, discovering new and obscure halal eateries, and contributing to the growing halal movement in the contemporary food industry.
